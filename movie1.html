The Godfather

The Godfather is a 1972 American crime film directed by Francis Ford Coppola, produced by Albert S. Ruddy, and based on Mario Puzo's best-selling 1969 novel of the same name. It is the first installment in The Godfather trilogy.

Main Actors:

  • Marlon Brando
  • Al Pacino
  • James Caan
